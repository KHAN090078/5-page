Located in the heartthrob city, Rawalpindi, the AGT Institute of Technical and Professional Education(AGT Tech) is a leading technical institute in Pakistan, committed to improving the human condition through advanced learning methodologies and technology. AGT (Arafat Group of Tirocinium) Tech is the brainchild of the visionary, educationist, philanthropist and entrepreneur, Malik Faisal Arafat. As a leading technical and professional institute, AGT Tech has more than 20state-of-the-art labs/workshops, focused on interdisciplinary courses and training that consistently contribute vital innovative approaches to the Pakistani government, industry, and business. For nearly ten years, AGT Tech has embraced the concept of endless possibilities in all leading technical trades. There is no doubt that AGT Tech stands out as a distinctively different kind of institute, one that is eagerly encouraging and developing the human capital of Pakistan in the light of revolutionary technologies of the 21st century. Equipped with the extremely rich resources and facilities, with an outstanding student community, faculty and support staff; strong partnerships / linkages with business, industry, government and Nonprofit organizations; and support from alumni and community, AGT Tech is designing a future of global dominance and leadership in providing skilled manpower to all leading firms/construction companies/other organizations.
